Note from the Author:

Greetings and salutations, fellow technology and gaming enthusiasts. My name is Michael Migliacio, and I am throwing my proverbial hat into the ring for a position at Code-Foo this year. I think the program is an excellent idea, speaking to individual talent and getting those with varying backgrounds actively involved in powering the information machine that makes up IGN. I've been a fan of IGN for a long time, and a hardcore gamer even longer - but let's not get wrapped up in numbers (unless, of course, those numbers happen to be related to ping-pong balls, school buses, liquid layout resolutions, or license plates). Please click through the application using the jQuery tabs at the top of the page, and if you have any questions don't hesitate to contact me!
